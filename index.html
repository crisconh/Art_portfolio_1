Antoni Alcover experiments with abstract forest representations, using engraving techniques to capture the intricate patterns and textures of nature. His work reflects the forest's shifting moods, inviting viewers to explore its depths through an abstract lens.
“I immerse myself in the forest's essence, letting its patterns and textures guide my hand. I choose my tools with intention, but the engraving process unfolds intuitively. The image emerges from the unknown, sometimes evoking the quiet depth of nature, and other times demanding attention with its bold, intricate details.”
